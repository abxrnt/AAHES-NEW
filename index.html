Frequently asked questions

How do I use the rank predictor?
Enter your expected rank, select categories if applicable, and review the predicted matches derived from historical closing ranks.
Where do you source cutoff data?
We aggregate published cutoffs from state university notices, college circulars, and verified student submissions — and flag the source for each entry.
Can I contribute PYQs or campus photos?
Yes — use our contact form to submit resources. We review contributions before publishing to maintain accuracy.
